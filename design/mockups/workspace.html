Product Vision

Engineering OS — an AI workspace that turns an idea into a production-grade repository.

Problem

Strong engineers ship work that under-sells them. The hard part of a great repository isn’t the code — it’s the discipline around it: defining the problem, recording decisions, keeping documentation honest.

Vision

A focused workspace that guides every project through the lifecycle great engineers already follow, using AI to remove friction — without making the decisions.

Why now

Models are finally good enough to draft and critique product and architecture artifacts, not just autocomplete code.

What success looks like

  • Idea → a documented, standards-compliant repository in under an hour.
  • The thinking artifacts are first-class and versioned, not throwaway chat.
